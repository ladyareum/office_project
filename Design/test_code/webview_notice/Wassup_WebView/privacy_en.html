The user’s social media (Facebook, Google, LINE, KakaoTalk, Apple, etc.) account information or email address, public profile of the corresponding account, gender, year of birth, and photos, etc., are collected when the user signs up for the service for the first time or in the process of using the service. 3. Period of Retention, Use and Destruction of Personal Information

  • For the service, the personal information is retained and processed during the period of retention and use of the personal information according to this policy and the related laws and regulations. Furthermore, the user's personal information will be destroyed without any delay except where it must be preserved in accordance with the laws and regulations and this policy, if and when the purpose of its collection and use has been achieved.     • Sign-up and management: until the user withdraws from membership

However, when necessary in order to handle and resolve disputes due to a wrongful use of the service in violation of laws and regulations or the terms and conditions of use, or to restrict the re-sign-up of unlawful users, the records on the use of the service and relevant supporting materials will be destroyed as per the above method after the user withdraws from membership, and they are preserved for 3 years.

7. Automatic Collection of Personal Information Through Cookies

The service uses “cookies” to provide specialized services and convenience to the users. Cookies are very small text files sent from the server used to operate the Company's website to the users’ browser, and are also stored on the hard disk within the users’ device.

  • Purpose of the use of cookies
    Cookies are used to enable convenient mutual communication for users based on the users’ login and recent records on access.
  • Right of users to refuse to allow cookies
    Users have the option to install cookies. This is configured via the web browser, with typical options being to allow all cookies, refuse all cookies, or to ask for permission each and every time cookies are saved. Please note that if the user chooses to refuse cookies, the user may face difficulties in using the service.
